# Creem Compliance Email Screenshot Guidelines

Below are detailed instructions on what screenshots to include in your Creem compliance email:

## 1. Overall Product Readiness Section

**Screenshots needed:**
- **Payment Flow**: Screenshot of the checkout page showing Creem payment options
- **Checkout Page**: Screenshot of the complete checkout process
- **Success Page**: Screenshot of the order confirmation/success page

**What to include:**
- Ensure the URL (nanobananalab.shop) is visible in the browser address bar
- Show the Creem payment button and integration clearly
- Demonstrate that the payment process flows smoothly

## 2. AI Wrapper Compliance Section

**Screenshots needed:**
- **Hero Section**: Full screenshot of the homepage hero section showing the mandatory disclaimer
- **Marketing Materials**: Any key marketing sections that were updated

**What to include:**
- The exact disclaimer text: "Nano Banana is an independent AI image editing platform..."
- Ensure no competitive claims (like "outperforms Flux Kontext") are visible
- Show the overall homepage header area

## 3. False Information Removal Section

**Screenshots needed:**
- **How It Works Guide**: Screenshot of the "How It Works" section that replaced testimonials
- **Updated FAQ**: Screenshot of the FAQ section showing focus on actual capabilities

**What to include:**
- The complete "How It Works" section with numbered steps
- Ensure no fake testimonials or misleading claims are present
- Show the FAQ section with questions and answers about actual product features

## 4. Reachable Customer Support Email Section

**Screenshots needed:**
- **Footer**: Screenshot of the website footer showing support email
- **Contact Information**: Any other visible contact information on the platform

**What to include:**
- The support@nanobananalab.shop email address clearly visible
- Footer links showing customer support access
- Any contact form or support section if available

## 5. Privacy Policy and Terms of Service Section

**Screenshots needed:**
- **Privacy Policy Page**: Full screenshot of the privacy policy page
- **Terms of Service Page**: Full screenshot of the terms of service page
- **Footer Links**: Screenshot showing the footer links to both legal pages

**What to include:**
- The URL of each legal page (e.g., nanobananalab.shop/privacy)
- Key sections like data collection policies, payment terms, and contact information
- The footer links labeled "Privacy Policy" and "Terms of Service"

## 6. Clear Pricing Display Section

**Screenshots needed:**
- **Pricing Cards**: Full screenshot of the pricing section showing all three plans
- **Plan Details**: Close-up of one of the pricing cards showing detailed features

**What to include:**
- Clear annual prices: Basic ($144/year), Pro ($234/year), Max ($480/year)
- All features listed under each plan (credits, commercial license, etc.)
- No hidden fees or misleading pricing information

## General Screenshot Guidelines

- **Browser**: Use a modern browser like Chrome or Firefox
- **Resolution**: Take screenshots at full screen resolution (1920x1080 recommended)
- **Visibility**: Ensure all relevant information is clearly visible and not cut off
- **URL**: Always show the full URL in the address bar
- **Quality**: Save screenshots as high-quality PNG files
- **Annotations**: If needed, add simple annotations to highlight key compliance elements

## How to Take Screenshots

- **Windows**: Press Windows + Shift + S to open the screenshot tool
- **Mac**: Press Command + Shift + 3 for full screen, Command + Shift + 4 for partial
- **Browser Extensions**: Use tools like Lightshot or Nimbus Screenshot for better control

## Where to Insert Screenshots in the Email

Insert each set of screenshots directly after the corresponding section in the email:
1. After "Overall Product Readiness" section
2. After "AI Wrapper Compliance" section  
3. After "False Information Removal" section
4. After "Reachable Customer Support Email" section
5. After "Privacy Policy and Terms of Service" section
6. After "Clear Pricing Display" section

By including these screenshots, you'll provide Creem support with clear, visual evidence that all compliance requirements have been met.
